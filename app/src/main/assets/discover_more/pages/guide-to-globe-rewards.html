Your Globe Rewards Are Now in the GlobeOne App!

Are you a long-time subscriber o bago palang sa Globe fam? Kung ano man ang status mo, here’s what you need to know: Signing on with Globe means you’re earning points you can collect, redeem and convert in a lot of exciting ways.

With Globe Rewards, simply being a Prepaid, Postpaid, Globe At Home or TM user makes you eligible for points that give you access to digital vouchers and let you score data promos. Here’s another thing to keep in mind, especially when sweldo comes around: You can use your points to shop at any of Globe’s partner brands. Enjoy steals and deals at places like The SM Store, BENCH/ and more!

Pero paano ba gamitin ang Globe Rewards? Ganito lang ‘yan:

How do rewards points work?

Earn points through when you use your Postpaid mobile or Broadband account, or keep topping up your Prepaid mobile or Prepaid WiFi devices! For Globe Prepaid reloads, the more you top up, the more you earn points. Just kick back, relax and keep using Globe.

Be sure to use up your points within one year to make sure they don’t expire!

Now, you can get your rewards on your GlobeOne app!

Check out your total points anytime, anywhere. The GlobeOne app lets you see your total points in real time, find available promos and partners and, of course, redeem your rewards: from Globe Promos for the best surfing experience to digital vouchers that can get you GCash Insurance, Jollibee treats, Lazada discounts and more. At ‘eto pa, rewards points can also be redeemed up to five times a day!

For work, play & bayanihan causes

With Globe Rewards, you can treat yourself to exclusive perks, but it’s also a great place to give back. In the spirit of bayanihan, you can support advocacies by donating your points to non-profit organizations like the Hineleban Foundation, Hopeline Philippines, PAWS Philippines and more.

This month, your rewards points can even help support the COVID-19 efforts of Philippine General Hospital (PGH) and help fill community pantries through the Walang Iwanan Alliance.

Super sulit na ngayon ang shopping, dining, travel at entertainment. Don’t miss out! Collect and redeem your Globe Rewards via the GlobeOne app.
