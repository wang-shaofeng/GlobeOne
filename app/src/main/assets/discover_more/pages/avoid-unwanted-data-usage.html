How You Can Avoid Unwanted Data Usage

Have you experienced that your PHP 500 load for the week didn’t last for three days? Magugulat ka na lang na back to zero na ‘yung load mo. Some might call it “nakaw load” or “kain load.” But in reality, our load is being used up by different call, text and surfing activities on our devices. ‘Di lang natin napapansin.

Kayang-kaya namang iwasan ang biglaang pagbawas ng load! Here are some reasons why you continue using up your data and load even when you’re not on your phone.

You have app in the background

Other apps can still use data even a fter you’re done using it. For example, TikTok continues to load videos in the background while you’re surfing on Facebook. Since TikTok is still working in the background, data and load are being used up without you realizing it. It’s easy to avoid your apps from consuming more load in three simple ways:

  • Completely exit the apps in the App Switcher on your phone.
  • Remember to turn off cellular data when you’re using WiFi.
  • Turn off your Background App Refresh options for Apple and Android gadgets.

Galing di ba! Paano? Ganito lang ‘yan:

How to turn off Background App Refresh for Apple / iOS

  • Go to Settings
  • Tap General
  • Tap Background App Refresh
  • Choose and toggle the apps you want to turn Background App Refresh on or off

How to turn off Background App Refresh for Android

  • Go to Settings
  • Tap Connections
  • Tap Data usage
  • Tap Mobile data usage
  • Choose and toggle the apps you allow or disallow for background data usage

You have an unpaid load loan

Load loans help us stay connected even in emergency purposes. Sometimes, we forget to pay for the and an overdue balance is still attached to your mobile number. So when you load your number again, it will automatically use this to pay your loan balance. It’s easy to check on your outstanding loan balances. Before loading your sim, check if you have due payments. Here’s how:

How to Check on Your Loan Balance

  • Log on to your GlobeOne App Tap
  • Tap on My Account to view your load loan balance

You are subscribed to a repeating value-added service or VAS

Load gets used up when you subscribe to a value-added service. VAS can be ringtones, wallpapers and mobile game tokens. For example, you forgot that you’re still subscribed to a ringback tone that still renews monthly. This consumes your load. But you can still unsubscribe to that by checking and canceling existing VAS subscriptions.

How to Check A VAS Subscription

Text HELP or CHECK to the 4-digit number you received a text from

How to Stop A VAS Subscription

Text STOP or UNSUBSCRIBE to the 4-digit number you received a text from

You have an unpaid load loan
