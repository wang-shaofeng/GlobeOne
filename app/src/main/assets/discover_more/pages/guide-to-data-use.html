Your Guide To Data Use: Ganito ‘Yan

Working from home and virtual learning have become our new reality, kaya hindi puwedeng mawalan ng mobile data. Even at home, dapat laging ready.

Ang tanong: ano bang favorite app mo, and how much data does it use?

Streaming Apps

Apps like YouTube, Netflix and Spotify use data when you’re watching and listening online. But how much?

Netflix

How much data is used while on Netflix depends on how long you watch and the quality of the video you’re watching:

  • 1-hour Standard Definition of content consumes 1 GB
  • 1-hour High Definition content consumes 3 GB

Youtube

With YouTube, it’s the quality of the video that really matters. You can adjust your video settings from low-, medium- and high-quality, depending on your available data. #SanaAll

  • 1-hour Low Quality (480p) video consumes 264 MB
  • 1-hour Medium Quality (720p) video consumes 870 MB
  • 1-hour High Quality (1080p) video consumes 1.65 GB

Spotify

If you’re streaming BLACKPINK’s Lovesick Girls or Olivia Rodrigo’s new album SOUR, whether you have a free or paid subscription, keep in mind: it all depends on your audio settings.

  • 3-minute song (Normal audio setting of approximately 96 kbps) consumes 2 MB
  • 3-minute song (High audio setting of approximately 160 kbps) consumes 3.5 MB
  • 3-minute song (Extreme audio setting of approximately 302 kbps) consumes 7.5 MB

Social Media Apps

Almost everyone is on social media. But do you know how much data is used when you scroll through your feed?

Instagram

Post, like and share!

  • 30-minute browsing consumes 300 MB to 450 MB
  • One IG photo or video consumes 0.5 MB
  • 0.5 MB One IG Story consumes 0.4 MB

Facebook

Connect with friends and family, o bili na Marketplace!

  • 30-minute browsing consumes 55 MB
  • 8-hour browsing with video content viewing consumes 1 GB

Twitter

Twitter allows users to stay up-to-date on the latest news and events, and the more time one spends scrolling, the more data is used.

  • 5-minute browsing consumes 30 MB
  • 30-minute browsing consumes 180 MB
  • 60-minute browsing consumes 360 MB
  • 90-minute browsing consumes 540 MB

Gaming Apps

For the kids, kids at heart or those with kids, below are some games and their corresponding data usage.

  • One (1) hour of Mobile Legends consumes about 2.5 MB
  • One (1) hour of Call of Duty consumes about 35 MB
  • One (1) hour of League of Legends consumes about 45 MB
  • One (1) hour of Minecraft consumes about 40 MB
  • One (1) hour of Roblox consumes anywhere between 100 to 300 MB

Productivity

With everyone at home, we’ve all had to make use of different productivity apps for our meetings, classes and everything else in between. But how much data do they consume?

Zoom

Have a meeting or want to catch up via e-numan session? Zoom is definitely the go-to.

  • Private calls between two people consume anywhere between 540 MB-1.62 GB
  • Group calls consume anywhere between 810 MB-2.4 GB

Google Meet and Google Classroom

Time to get down to business!

  • Private calls between two people consume 810 MB for those that join and 1.4 GB for those that host
  • Group calls with 5 joining participants consume 1.4 GB
  • Group calls with 10 joining participants 1.8 GB

Google Docs, Slides, Sheets, etc.

Working on a presentation or report with other teammates?

  • Consume about 0.2-0.5 Mbps per session
